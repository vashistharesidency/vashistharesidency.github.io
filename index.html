About Us

Welcome to Vashistha Residency

Located in the peaceful town of Bhadrachalam, close to the famous Sri Rama Temple, Vashistha Residency is the perfect place for your stay. We offer a calm and comfortable environment, ideal for both pilgrims and those seeking a quiet getaway.
Our residency is designed to give you the best of both worlds—traditional warmth and modern comfort. Being near the Sri Rama Temple, you can easily experience the spiritual atmosphere of Bhadrachalam.
We invite you to stay with us and enjoy a home-like experience in this sacred town.
